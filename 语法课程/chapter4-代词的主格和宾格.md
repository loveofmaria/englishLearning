### 代词的主格和宾格



##### 主格代词：I	he	she	it	you	we	they  [ 用来做主语的代词 ]

- **I** am a teacher.
- **He** is a teacher.
- **You**  are a teacher.
- **They**  are a teacher.



##### 宾格代词：me	him	her	it	you	us	them 【用来做宾语的代词】

- He likes **me**.
- We like **her**.
- I like **them**.



##### 代词练习：

- 他喜欢她/他/它。
- 他们认识他/她/它。
