### 反身代词【表示自己】



myself 	yourself	herself	himself	itself yourselves	ourselves	themselves



#### 反身代词的用法

- **放在动词之后做宾语**

  - Please help **yourself** to some fish. 

  - We enjoyed **ourselves** last night. 

- **做主语的同位语【和主语并列】**

  - The thing **itself** is not important.
